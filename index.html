01 — About Me

Numbers never lied to me.
People needed someone
to explain them.

My journey started in an accounting classroom — columns, ledgers, the quiet logic of balance sheets. But I quickly discovered that the most interesting question wasn't what the numbers said. It was why, and what to do next.

That curiosity led me to data analytics. I taught myself Power BI, mastered DAX and Power Query, and began turning messy spreadsheets into dashboards that actually help people make decisions. At DEPI, I built systems that cut reporting time by 25% and streamlined workflows by 30%. At CIB Bank, I saw first-hand how financial data drives real-time strategy.

Then came the part that changed everything: coaching. Through ITIDA's Gigs Program, I mentored over 300 early-career freelancers, reviewed more than 3,000 proposals, and watched people land their first international clients because they finally knew how to tell their story in data — and in a proposal.

Today I sit at the intersection of two worlds: the analyst who reads data, and the coach who reads people. I'm passionate about helping organizations make smarter decisions and helping individuals build careers they're proud of.
